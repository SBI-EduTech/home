Digital Courses by SBI EduTech

SBI EduTech offers an extensive range of Digital Courses tailored to meet the educational and career needs of students from diverse academic backgrounds. These courses are specially designed to provide practical knowledge, enhance technical skills, and increase employability in various industries. Students enrolled in programs like BA, BSc, BCom, BCA, MCA, BSc IT, MSc IT, BTech, MTech, and related disciplines can greatly benefit from these offerings. Upon successful completion, participants receive a certificate, adding value to their resumes.

Key Features of Digital Courses:

1. Comprehensive Curriculum:
Each course is structured to cover foundational concepts, advanced topics, and practical applications. Industry-relevant skills are integrated to ensure students are job-ready.

2. Interactive Learning:
Courses include video lectures, live sessions, and interactive quizzes, making the learning experience engaging and effective.

3. Project-Based Approach:
Students get hands-on experience by working on real-world projects, helping them apply theoretical knowledge to practical problems.

4. Expert Instructors:
The courses are designed and delivered by experienced educators and industry professionals, ensuring high-quality learning material.

5. Flexible Learning:
Students can access course materials anytime, anywhere, making it easier for them to learn at their own pace.

6. Certification:
Upon successful completion of a course, students receive an industry-recognized certificate from SBI EduTech, which can boost their career prospects.

A) Course Categories
SBI EduTech’s Digital Courses cover a wide range of topics, including but not limited to:

1. Technical Skills
- Programming Languages: C, Python, Java, C++, etc.
- Web Development: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, Angular.
- Data Science & Analytics: Power BI, Tableau, Machine Learning, Data Visualization, Big Data, PySpark.
- Cloud Computing: DevOps, AWS, Azure, Google Cloud.
- Database Management: SQL, MongoDB.
- Others: Adv Excel, SAP ABAP,Servicenow, Automation Testing, IFS-FSM.

2. Professional Skills
- Business Communication.
- Resume Building and Interview Preparation.
- Teamwork and Leadership Skills.

3. Domain-Specific Courses
- Financial Accounting and Tally for BCom students.
- IT Tools and Applications for BCA and MCA students.
- Engineering Design Software (AutoCAD, MATLAB) for BTech and MTech students.
- Research Methodology for MSc IT and similar programs.

4. Soft Skills Development
- Public Speaking.
- Time Management.
- Problem-Solving Techniques.

B) Benefits for Students
- Enhancement of Skills: The courses are curated to fill the gap between academic learning and industry requirements, preparing students for a competitive job market.
- Personalized Guidance: Students receive guidance and mentorship from professionals.
- Networking Opportunities: Engaging with instructors and peers can lead to valuable connections in their respective fields.
- Career Advancement: Certified courses give students an edge in job interviews and career growth.

C) Certification Details
The certification provided by SBI EduTech serves as proof of the skills and knowledge acquired during the course. It includes:
- The name of the course.
- The participant’s name.
- A unique certification ID.
- The date of completion.
- Authentication from SBI EduTech.
