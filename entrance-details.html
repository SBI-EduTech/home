Entrance Exam Guidance by SBI EduTech

SBI EduTech offers comprehensive guidance and support for students preparing for various entrance exams, including academic and IT-based government exams. Our expert-curated content and resources aim to help students achieve their career aspirations by providing them with the right tools and strategies. Below are the details of the services we provide for different exams:

A) Academic Entrance Exams

1. NET JRF (National Eligibility Test - Junior Research Fellowship)
- Guidance on Eligibility and Syllabus: Detailed understanding of subject-specific syllabi for various disciplines.
- Study Material: Expert-curated study notes, previous years' question papers, and model tests.
- Mock Tests: Online and offline mock tests that simulate the real exam environment.
- Strategy Development: Personalized study plans and time management techniques.
- Interview Preparation: Guidance for candidates who qualify for JRF interviews.

2. NTA CUET (Common University Entrance Test)
- Syllabus Coverage: Comprehensive notes and video lectures covering all subjects.
- Practice Questions: Topic-wise and full-length practice tests to build confidence.
- Guided Doubt Clearing: Regular doubt-solving sessions with experts.
- Time Management Tips: Techniques to maximize efficiency during the exam.

3. JEE CUP (Joint Entrance Examination for Polytechnic)
- Technical Subjects: Extensive resources for mathematics, physics, chemistry, and technical aptitude.
- Problem-Solving Sessions: Special classes focused on numerical problem-solving.
- Previous Yearâ€™s Analysis: Insights into past question papers and common trends.
- Preparation Schedule: Customized weekly schedules to track progress.

4. LU MBA (Lucknow University MBA Entrance)
- Simulated Exams: Practice tests replicating company-specific placement patterns.
- Coding Challenges: Competitive programming sessions to improve problem-solving speed and accuracy.
- Real-Time Feedback: Personalized reports highlighting strengths and areas of improvement.

B) IT-Based Government Exams

1. GIC (General Insurance Corporation) Exams
- Technical Knowledge: Specialized content for IT and programming-related topics.
- General Awareness: Coverage of insurance-related current affairs and policies.
- Reasoning and Aptitude: Targeted practice for logical reasoning and numerical aptitude sections.

2. PO (Probationary Officer)
- Quantitative Aptitude and Reasoning: In-depth practice for numerical and logical sections.
- English Proficiency: Focus on grammar, comprehension, and communication skills.
- Mock Tests: Sectional and full-length mock tests with detailed analysis.

3. UPSC Assistant Programmer
- Programming Skills: Tutorials on key programming languages such as C, Python, Java, and C++.
- System Analysis and Design: Notes and video lessons on system design principles.
- Mock Coding Tests: Regular assessments to improve coding efficiency.
- General Studies: Coverage of basic general knowledge and current affairs.

4. Other IT-Based Government Exams
- Comprehensive Study Material: Resources covering both technical and non-technical aspects.
- Real-Time Practice: Online platforms to practice programming and technical aptitude tests.
- Exam Updates: Regular notifications about upcoming exams, eligibility criteria, and application deadlines.

Target Audience
